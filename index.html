Loid Forger - Codename : Twilight

Loid Forger aka agent "Twilight" had served as a spy for at least ten years after serving in the Westalian military for a few years. His current mission moves him to Ostania where he creates the alias "Loid Forger," and starts the Forger Family, becoming the adoptive father of Anya and entering a fake marriage with Yor Briar. Loid can be considered a jack of all trades: he is extremely physically and mentally skilled, has a razor-sharp mind and intellect, and is able to adapt to nearly any situation, only ever surpassed combat-wise by stronger individuals like Yor or matched by other agents of similar caliber. His skillset includes proficiency in hand-to-hand combat, marksmanship, and mastery in disguises.

Yor Forger - Codename : Thorn Princess

Yor Forger works as an ordinary office clerk at Berlint City Hall, while also leading a secret life as a Garden assassin under the code name "Thorn Princess". Needing a cover to continue her job as an assassin, Yor enters a fake marriage with Loid Forger with the intention of fulfilling both of their respective goals and subsequently becomes the adoptive mother of his daughter, Anya. Despite her slim frame, Yor possesses monstrously superhuman physical abilities. Her punches and kicks are strong enough to generate mild but noticeable air currents.

Anya Forger - formerly known as : Test Subject 007

She is the adopted daughter of Loid and Yor Forger. She attends Eden Academy as a first-year student in Cecile Hall. Anya, formerly known as Test Subject 007 is also a telepath whose abilities were created in an experiment conducted by an unknown organization. Anya is capable of reading the minds of other people and animals; her ability depicted as small sparks around her head. Due to her telepathy skills, unbeknownst to her adoptive parents, she knows what both of her parents real jobs are, despite their efforts to hide it from her.

Bond Forger - formerly known as : Subject 8

Bond is the pet dog of the Forger Family. Formerly known as Subject 8, Bond was used as a test subject in the Ostanian research project known as Project Apple, which resulted in him gaining precognitive abilities. Bond's time as a research experiment left him with precognitive abilities and above-average intelligence for a dog. He is capable of understanding human speech. Bond gained precognitive abilities from the experiments done to him, allowing him to briefly see sometimes fragmented scenes from a few minutes to a few hours into the future.
